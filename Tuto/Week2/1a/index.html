Massey University - Programmes and Papers

Programmes and papers

Programmes are the qualifications (e.g. certificates, diplomas, degrees) that you can study at the University. Programmes are made up of papers.

academic year

The academic year is divided into two semesters followed by Summer School. Papers can be taught during one semester or over two semesters.

locations

You can study papers internally on the Albany, Manawatu and Wellington campuses as well as by distance learning (extramurally).

We offer over 200 programmes so whatever you are looking for it is likely that Massey University as the largest university in New Zealand, offers it.
