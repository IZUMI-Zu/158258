THE URBAN EXPLORATION FALL/WINTER 2022

"Urban Ecology" Capsule Debuts in Continuation of the Theme "In City. In Nature."

Whether in the city or out in nature, exploration is never out of reach. The "Urban Ecology" capsule is a continuation of Our Urban Exploration "In City. In Nature." theme that brings new light to how we view greenery in everyday life. Moving away from the concrete jungle, the new capsule inspires us to seek creative ways of living and explores the close relationship between the city and nature to discover a new kind of urban exploration.

Unlimited Exploration

Freedom is everywhere, from the top of the mountain to the city streets. Our's new "Urban Ecology" capsule thoughtfully intertwines inspiration from the outdoors with everyday wear to open up an in-depth exploration between humans, nature and the city. This season’s capsule design echoes the natural world with functional and aesthetic pieces made with ultra-lightweight fabrics. Featuring a multi-pocket design, the D1 City Vest also comes equipped with a detachable panel that can provide storage and style at the same time.

The innovative D1 City Wind Vest is made of water-repelling and wind-proof WindWall fabric with a hidden button design on three-dimensional patch pockets. It can be layered with the D1 City Wind Jacket for an easy transition between urban and outdoor styles. The new vest jackets come in neutral earth tones with a unique design that takes into account the needs of multiple lifestyles as Our hopes to provide explorers with functional and fashionable equipment that allows them to travel freely between different environments and explore endless possibilities.

Ready for the Change of Seasons

The new jackets from the "Urban Ecology" capsule show a perfect fusion of design, functionality and innovation, while interpreting the interaction between nature and the city Equipped with waterproof, moisture-wicking and wear-resistant features, the D2 Utility Dryden Jacket made with Dryden fabric inherits the silhouette of the classic Mountain 2000 with outdoor design details such as a carabiner and climbing ropes.

Together with the D2 City Casual Pants with Wind-wall and DWR water repellent protection, this style combination can help explorers face the ever-changing environment with ease. Featuring an A-line fit, the women’s D1 City SS Tee uses vibrant stitching elements and the same waterproof and moisture-wicking Dryden fabric for the ultimate in style and comfort. Customize your look with an adjustable drawstring hat to showcase a sleek and chic look.
