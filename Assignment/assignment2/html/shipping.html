worldwide Orders

Deliveries outside of the China may take anywhere from 7-30 days to arrive,
depending on the destination country.

These times may be delayed by a number of factors,

but you have not yet received it, please call your local post office for more information.

they may need to get in touch with you before delivery can be completed.
